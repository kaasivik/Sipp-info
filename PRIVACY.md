# Privacy Policy for Sipp

**Last updated:** January 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how **Sipp** ("we", "us", or "the App") handles your information. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

### 1. Data Collection and Usage
Sipp is designed to be a private experience. We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal information, location data, or usage analytics on our servers.

### 2. Apple Health (HealthKit) Integration
Sipp integrates with Apple's Health app to track your water intake.

* **Reading Data:** We read your water intake data from the Health app to display your hydration history.
* **Writing Data:** We write water intake data to the Health app when you log a drink within Sipp.

**Privacy of Health Data:**
* Sipp **does not** use your Health data for advertising, marketing, or use-based data mining.
* Your Health data is never shared with third parties.
* Your Health data remains on your device (and your personal iCloud, if enabled by you).

### 3. Data Storage
All data generated or processed by Sipp is stored locally on your device. If you have iCloud backups or iCloud Drive enabled, your data may be stored in your personal iCloud account, which is managed by Apple and subject to Apple's privacy policies. We have no access to this data.

### 4. Third-Party Services
Sipp does not include any third-party analytics SDKs or advertising networks.

### 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

### 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, please [contact us](https://support.uomagrit.com/sipp).
